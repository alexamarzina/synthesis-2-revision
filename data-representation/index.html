Data Representation

Drumroll please...

Popular music changes a lot over time, probably because what is "popular" in general changes quite often. What seemed to be a consistent trend in music, however, seemed to be the theme of love. I originally investigated this through utilizing the Million Song Dataset in February 2017. I produced a wordcloud displaying the most frequently used words in song titles from about 1930 to 2010.


I was then curious about if this was a constant trend or something that changed over time, so I made a line graph depicting the frequencies of the word "love" in song titles that were released in each year.


(I recommend clicking on "show closest data on hover" in the top toolbar of the graph, so you can see both the year and number of "loves" when you hover over different parts of the graph.)
